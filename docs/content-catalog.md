# WorkLang AI — Content Catalog

This catalog is the duplicate-prevention inventory and curriculum plan for
lesson authors. Read it together with
[`content-guidelines.md`](content-guidelines.md) before creating content, and
append every newly created lesson in the same change.

Lesson numbers are sequential within each language and career track. The
professional topic summarizes the scenario used across the lesson rather than
merely repeating the career-track name.

# Curriculum Rules

Lessons 11–20 continue the same lesson architecture as lessons 1–10.

Every lesson must include:

- grammar theory;
- approximately 15 vocabulary items;
- professional reading text;
- deterministic exercises;
- LLM-supported exercises;
- writing task;
- original content only.

Grammar topics may repeat across career tracks within the same language, but
professional scenarios, vocabulary, reading texts, exercises, and writing tasks
must remain unique for each career track.

Future curriculum blocks should continue in groups of ten lessons:

- Lessons 1–10: Foundations
- Lessons 11–20: Advanced Business Communication
- Lessons 21–30: Professional Negotiation & Leadership (planned)
- Lessons 31–40: Executive & Consulting Communication (planned)
